      \textbf{Explanation:}
      \begin{itemize}
          \item Break down each digit by its place value.
          \item $9$ is in the hundreds place: $900$.
          \item $1$ is in the tens place: $10$.
          \item $5$ is in the ones place: $5$.
          \item Combine: $900 + 10 + 5$.
      \end{itemize}
    }{}
